About Our App

Dots Trio has developed "Ludo Tale". Ludo Tale is a mobile based exciting gaming application which gives you an amazing experience. As the name suggests, game provides a solid pinch of traditional ludo and new levels plus goals based format. Along with nice graphics and class gameplay it will help you with a soothing music for an extremely pleasing gaming experience. It comes up with a never-before superior features and bundles of new levels packed with unknown challenges everytime. Become a NEW-GEN Ludo warrior and finish the TALE only with Ludo Tale.

Amazing Gameplay

Experience top-class graphics and gameplay. Build your strategies, counter attack enemy and feel like a warrior, all this with superior display and amazing User Experience.

Exciting Levels

Enjoy numeorus levels with every level coming up with unkonwn challenges. Strech your muscles and try to make a difference with this new format Ludo. Enjoy challenges and goals.

Stylish Design

Feel the true ludo experience with super stylish design. Experience the cutting edge design and enhance your performance. Highly fascinating design to enlighten your mood.

Data Backup

Store your progress data as a backup. You can save data on every 10 levels(checkpoints), so that even if you uninstall the app, you can resume it from last saved checkpoint.

Unconventional Ludo

Ludo Tale has some base deriving from conventional ludo but most of it is unconventional. You need to chase complete different sets of goals in every level.

Life Challenges

Everytime you play a game you lose a life and post some time you may need help from Life Pot or Luck game to recover your lives. Yes, gaming lives too have values!
